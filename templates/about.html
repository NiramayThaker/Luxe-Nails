Our Story

Passion, Precision, and Pink.

How It Started

LuxeNails was born from a desire to elevate the standard nail salon experience into something truly extraordinary. Founded in 2024 by Isabella Rose, a master nail artist with over 15 years of experience, our studio was conceived as a sanctuary where art meets self-care.

Isabella started in a small room in her apartment, fueled by an obsession with perfect cuticles and innovative designs. Her reputation for perfection quickly spread, leading to the opening of our flagship studio in Beverly Hills.

What Inspires Us

A Brief History of Nails

Honoring the tradition of beauty.

3000 BC

In ancient China, nail color indicated social status. Royals wore gold and silver, later evolving to black and red.

19th Century

The "almond" shape became popular, and oils/creams were used for shine before liquid polish existed.

1920s

The moon manicure (painting only the middle of the nail) was the height of fashion.

Today

Nail art has become a global phenomenon, blending technology with pure artistic talent.
